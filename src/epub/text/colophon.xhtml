La Firmao de la Kato Kiu Pilkludas
was published in 1830 as La Maison du chat-qui-pelote by
Honoré de Balzac.
It was translated from French in 1924 by
Paul Benoit.

This ebook was produced
by
Paul King
and is based on a transcription produced in 2022 by
Andrew Sly, Miroslav Malovec, and The Online Distributed Proofreading Team
for
Project Gutenberg
and on digital scans from
Wikimedia Commons.

The cover page is adapted from
The House of the Cat and Racket,
an illustration completed in 1896 by
Édouard Toudouze.
The cover and title pages feature the
League Spartan and Sorts Mill Goudy
typefaces created in 2014 and 2009 by
The League of Moveable Type.

The first edition of this ebook was released on
October 7, 2023, 3:29 p.m.
